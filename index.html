The Batman who laughs

The Batman Who Laughs is a character from DC Comics, first introduced in Dark Nights: Metal #2 (2017), created by writer Scott Snyder and artist Greg Capullo.
He is an alternate version of Batman from the Dark Multiverse, a dark and twisted dimension in the DC universe. His origin and characteristics make him one of the most terrifying and compelling villains in the Batman mythos.

Origin and creation

The Batman Who Laughs is an alternate version of Bruce Wayne from Earth-22. In this version, Bruce Wayne is pushed to the brink when he is forced to kill the Joker, his greatest enemy, in self-defense.
However, after killing the Joker, he is exposed to a toxin from the Joker's body that transforms him. This toxin causes him to gain the Joker's insanity while also retaining his brilliant mind and physical prowess as Batman.
This version of Batman is driven by a new, horrific purpose: a twisted combination of his detective skills and Joker-like chaos.
He becomes a dark reflection of the hero he once was, twisted by the same motivations and methods he once fought against. As a result, he gains an unpredictable and sadistic nature, with a deep love for violence and psychological manipulation.
